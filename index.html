01 — Mission

I believe discipline is worship. Every rep, every mile, every early morning is an offering.

I'm Max Mancuso — known online as Cooseing. I'm a HYROX PRO athlete, online coach, and content creator based in Charleston, SC. My mission is to help others find strength — physical, mental, and spiritual — through intentional training and unwavering faith.

Training isn't separate from worship. It's how I honor what I've been given. Whether I'm racing the HYROX circuit, running the streets of Charleston, or coaching athletes through RevitalizeU, the mission is the same: become stronger so you can serve better.

"Not only so, but we also glory in our sufferings, because we know that suffering produces perseverance; perseverance, character; and character, hope."

— Romans 5:3–5
